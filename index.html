Our objectives

To supplement Government effort of providing quality education to all children in Uganda. To provide employment opportunities to teachers in the area who could not be employed by Government of Uganda. To propagate (transmit from one generation to the next) values of Adventist Education to children in the area.

Our Mission

To nurture children in Christian philosophy of honesty, self-reliance, simple lifestyle and service to the community.

Our Strength

The school has a Primary Leaving Examination Center number. The school is legally registered by the Ministry of Education & Sports of Uganda. We have a small ratio of 35 students pupils to every teacher per class. All teachers are qualified and go through regular refresher courses.

Our Vision

A Seventh-day Adventist Church affiliated school, geared producing self-reliant citizens in life here and thereafter.
