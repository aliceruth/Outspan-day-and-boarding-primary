ABOUT US

Outspan Primary School is a Seventh-day Adventist Church affiliated educational Institution located along Gayaza-Zirobwe road 25 km. from the nation's capital Kampala, at Namulonge Trading Center, Nansana Municipality in Wakiso District. The School started in September of 1999 using rented premises. Now over the more than 20 years after inception, the School has so far been blessed with a long fruitful stay in the area and with a school population of 1,200 learners and the number of staff growing to over 80 employees

The school is legally registered by the Ministry of Education & Sports of the Republic of Uganda and has got a Primary Leaving Examination center number. Apart from being an academic Institution, the school endeavours to equip learners with skills or talent developing programmes; namely children are trained to play brass band music, since the school possesses a brass band, learners are encouraged to appreciate the use of their hands, head and heart as part of their education particularly in engaging in diary zero grazing, poultry and goat rearing, games and sports and crafts (needle work) and many other activities.
